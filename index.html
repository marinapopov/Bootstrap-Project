About Us

Craving some delicious breakfast? Maybe you’re in the mood for a croissant? No matter what kind of meal
you have in mind, The Familia Restaurant is ready to prepare it for you.

Our ovens and bakery are the center of our kitchen starting from the pastries in the morning, freshly baked breads
for sandwiches and salads at lunch, to the bread to sop up your chicken at dinner. Our bread, all made from
locally sourced grains, is baked fresh on site daily.

We are open daily from 7 a.m. to 11 p.m. We’re here to serve you the best food around,
whenever you’re looking for a great American restaurant in New York.
